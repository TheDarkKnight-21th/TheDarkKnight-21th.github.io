About

Hello, I am Jungmyung.

I am a graduate student researcher at Korea University. My current research focuses on improving compositional text-to-image generation by designing structured reward signals and efficient reinforcement learning strategies for diffusion and flow-based models.

Research Area

Text-to-image generation, diffusion models, and multimodal alignment.

Current Focus

Reward optimization for multi-concept and compositional generation.

Affiliation

Korea University.

Research

Making generative models follow complex prompts.

My work studies how generative models can satisfy multiple visual concepts simultaneously, including objects, attributes, numeracy, spatial relations, and fine-grained bindings. I am especially interested in reward design, preference alignment, and scalable training methods for high-quality visual generation.
